About Studious Ask

Primary

Studious Ask is a messaging platform built by students, for students. Whether you're working on group projects, need help with homework, or just want to connect with classmates, Studious Ask is your go-to space.

🎓 What You Can Do

  • 📚 Ask and answer academic questions
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Collaborate with classmates in group chats
  • 📝 Share notes, resources, and updates
  • 📅 Stay organized with study groups and planning tools

💡 Why We Built It

We believe communication is the key to better learning. Our mission is to make it easier for students to recieve help from AI agents — no matter the subject, grade level, or timezone.
